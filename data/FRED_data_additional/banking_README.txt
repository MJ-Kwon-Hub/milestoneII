ALLACBM027NBOG                                                        

Title
----------------------------------------------------------------------
LESS: Allowance for Loan and Lease Losses, All Commercial Banks       

Source
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (US)                 

Release
----------------------------------------------------------------------
H.8 Assets and Liabilities of Commercial Banks in the United States   

Units
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Billions of U.S. Dollars                                              

Frequency
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Monthly                                                               

Seasonal Adjustment
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Not Seasonally Adjusted                                               

Notes
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Allowance for Loan and Lease Losses is a contra asset account. A      
contra asset account is an item that is entered on the asset side of  
the balance sheet of a corporation or entity even though the item has 
a credit or negative balance.                                         
                                                                      
For further information, please refer to the Board of Governors of the
Federal Reserve System's H.8 release, online at                       
http://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/h8/.
